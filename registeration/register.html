Technical Program Registration

The AAAI-26 technical program registration includes admission to all technical paper and poster sessions, invited talks, the AAAI Conference on Innovative Applications of Artificial Intelligence (IAAI-26), the Symposium on Educational Advances in Artificial Intelligence (EAAI-26), exhibits, demos, the opening reception, and access to AAAI-26/IAAI-26/EAAI-26 papers via the conference schedule and app. Workshop, tutorial, bridge, and lab registration, as well as other special programs, are available for an additional fee. At least one author is required to register for the conference at the in-person rate and to present the paper in-person, and we encourage all authors to attend if possible.
