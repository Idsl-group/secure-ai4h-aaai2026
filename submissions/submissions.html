Format of Workshop

SECURE-AI4H will feature a highly interactive and engaging program spanning the full 7-day schedule. The format includes keynote talks by global leaders in AI for health and regulation, peer-reviewed papers and poster presentations, and interactive panels.

Attendance

Invited speakers from academia and industry, along with authors of peer-reviewed accepted papers, will present their work through oral and poster sessions.

Topics

Submissions are invited on topics including but not limited to:

  • Adversarial Robustness in Medical AI
  • Uncertainty Quantification in Clinical Decisions
  • Explainable AI for Clinical End-Users
  • Fairness and Bias Mitigation Across Demographics
  • Privacy-Preserving Healthcare AI
  • Regulatory Pathways and Certification Frameworks
  • Clinical Validation and Real-World Evidence
  • Robust Medical Imaging Under Distribution Shifts
  • Safety-Critical Decision Support Systems
  • Secure Multi-Modal Health Data Integration
  • Human-AI Collaboration in Clinical Settings
  • Ethical AI for Vulnerable Populations
  • Generative AI in Healthcare with Safety Constraints
  • Formal Verification and Theoretical Guarantees
  • End-to-End Deployment and MLOps for Healthcare
